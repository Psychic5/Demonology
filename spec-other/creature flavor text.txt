- Imps -
Where Imps lack in political and social power, they make up for it in their pyromancy skills. A single Imp may be powerful enough to kill a skilled adventurer, if caught by surprise. One must tread carefully through extreme environments, where Imps are most interested in settingn up camp.

- Corkscrew -
Corkscrews are biomechanical amalgams posessed by a dark spirit. Legend has it that an ancient civilization built them, only for them to go rogue and turn on their creators. However, there is no evidence to back this up... The metal that Corkscrews are made up of is highly sought out by engineers and smiths, for its obscene, yet handy properties.
