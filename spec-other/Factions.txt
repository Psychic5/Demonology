- Heretics -
A faction of Illagers that dedicate their time to harnessing the dark magicks of the world. They are heavily misguided in their world views, thinking the darkness to be power up for grabs, rather than its true nature as a natural, balanced force. Often the Heretics perform dark rituals in attempts to summon a powerful demon. Thus far, said rituals have never succeeded, what with the Heretics being so ignorant in the field of magic. However their most recent attempts have grown dangerously close, only due to their sheer dumb luck. The spell is incomplete however, with the final step still being up to interpretation...
